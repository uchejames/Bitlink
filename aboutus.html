ABOUT BITLINK

Bitlink Technologies is a cryptocurrency exchange that offers the safest way to buy and sell bitcoin,USDT ETH and also send, receive and store your crypto with your bitcoin wallet on Bitlink . Bitlink offers digital currency exchange for the cash market that focuses on making bitcoin and basic financial services more accessible. Bitlink is powering the cryptocurrency ecosystem in Africa. Bitlink is in 16 countries and rapidly building a Pan-African crypto network. Bitlink enables anyone on the continent to buy, sell, spend, and store Bitcoin, Ethereum, or USDT to solve basic financial service issues. Serving Cameroon, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, Botswana, Rwanda, Tanzania, Ghana, Zambia, Uganda, Gabon, Malawi, and more coming.
